Meet The Team:

WhoDis? is a fun, quiz game where your knowledge of internet culture, obscure references, and unforgettable (or completely forgettable) characters is put to the test. From iconic internet sounds like Tung Tung Tung Sahur to Trippi Troppi — this quiz is all about testing how deep your brainrot truly goes. It’s weird, it’s funny, and it might just call you out a little. Built for the chronically online and the casually curious, WhoDis? is your new favorite way to waste five minutes and leave wondering, “Wait… why do I know this?".
